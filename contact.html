House Etiquette

01. The Rule of Whispers

The Grand Library is a strict quiet zone. Please take calls to the Café or the exterior courtyard.

02. Digital Disconnect

Laptops are permitted only in designated areas of The Inkwell Café. Reading rooms are analog.

03. Outside Fare

To protect our collection, outside food is not permitted. The Inkwell is happy to serve you.
